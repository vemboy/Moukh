Moukh, our family dog, is a gampr, a large-bodied Armenian shepheard, playful and earnest but very vigilant. His name means smoke, matching the colors of his coat: black and gray with white accents. He is twice the size of his mother Noush. Whenever they are not in the city, they roam the Papazian vineyards together.
